Overview

This document leverages the growthbreaks R package to investigate potential spatio-temporal breaks, or clusters, in pacific cod growth across the range. There are some major caveats in analyzing these data wholesale, largely due to sampling changes through time – so this investigation is intended to guide discussions and inference, not to be the final word on spatial demographic structure. This uses SPECIMEN data from all three regions.
